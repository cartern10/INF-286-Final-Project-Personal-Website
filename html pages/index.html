Bio



Nickolas Carter was born and raised in a small town of Hawesville Kentucky. He would spend 18 years in the small town growing and learning. He found himself interested in computers as he grew up particually with the security and vulnerabilities computers had. He would eventually get accepted into college at Murray State University. Here he would pursue a bachelor's degree in cybersecurity. During his studies he also took part in clubs such as ATSM and Murray State Esports Club. Highlighting his time in the Murray State Esports club where he would join a competitive eports team becoming a member of their Tom Clany's Rainbow Six Siege team. After two years at MSU, Nick would go on to transfer at the start of his junior year to Northern Kentucky University, where he still attends, still pursuing a bachelor's in cybersecurity.

His current short-term goals are to complete his degree in cybersecurity and join the work force in the next 2 years.
